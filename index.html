Chihuahua

Chihuahua is the largest state of Mexico. It is located in the northwestern part of the country and is bordered by the states of Sonora to the west, Sinaloa to the southwest, Durango to the south and Coahuila to the east. To the north and northeast, it shares an extensive border with U.S. The largest city is Ciudad Juarez and the capital city is Chihuahua.

The famous Mexican train "CHEPE" starts from Chihuahua, and reaches the Pacific Ocean, through the Sierra Madre and the Copper Canyon.
